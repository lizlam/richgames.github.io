How to Play French Military Game

20 Feb 2017

French Military Game is a 19th century game that was popular among French officers. It is played with just 4 pieces: 3 hunters and 1 prey. Also known as Hare & Hounds, it is one of the smallest hunt games. Mathematician Martin Gardneri, writer of monthly column “Mathematical Games” in Scientific American stated that the game “combines extreme simplicity with extraordinary strategic subtley.”

Step 2: Learn the Rules

  • Hunters starts first.
  • Hunters may move (one piece at a time) 1 space horizontally to the right or vertically (never backwards and never diagonally)
  • The prey may move 1 step in any direction along a line (horizontal, vertical or diagonal)

Hunters wins by trapping the prey so that it can no longer move on its turn.
Prey wins by passing all the hunters and reaching the other end of the board.

Step 3: Switch

After the first game switch sides and play again.
